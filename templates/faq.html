What is the purpose of this website?

This website helps users create, manage, and organize tasks. It allows users to categorize tasks, set priorities, deadlines, and track the status of each task.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, go to the Register page and fill out the required details. After successfully registering, you can log in using your credentials.

How do I add a new task?

To start creating tasks, follow these simple steps:

  1. Log in: Ensure you are logged in to your account.
  2. Go to the Task Page: Once logged in, navigate to the "My Tasks" page using the navigation bar.
  3. Click on 'Create New Task': You will find a "Create New Task" button on the task list page.
  4. Fill in the Task details.
  5. Submit the Task: Click the "Submit" button to save your task.

Start creating tasks here!

Can I filter my tasks?

Yes, you can filter tasks based on priority, category, and status using the filter options at the top of the task list.

How do I update or delete a task?

Each task in your list has an "Update" and "Delete" button. Click "Update" to modify the task details or "Delete" to remove the task from your list.
Update or Delete your tasks here!

What happens when I delete a task?

Once you confirm the deletion of a task, it will be permanently removed from your task list and cannot be recovered.
